Privacy Policy for CalendarGPT

Last updated: June 24, 2025

CalendarGPT is a web-based conversational assistant that allows users to manage their Google Calendar using natural language chat.

This privacy policy applies to the application CalendarGPT, hosted at https://calendargpt.org/.

AI/ML Disclosure: CalendarGPT uses AI models solely to interpret the user's natural language input in order to fulfill their explicit calendar-related requests. For example, when a user writes "schedule a meeting with Sarah tomorrow at 10am", the AI model helps convert that sentence into a structured event for the Google Calendar API.

The AI engine only processes the user's typed prompts; it does not access or store any calendar data beyond what is required to execute the request. CalendarGPT does not use any Google user data to train or improve its AI models. No calendar information, contact details, or event metadata are ever used as training data, nor are they logged outside the session.
